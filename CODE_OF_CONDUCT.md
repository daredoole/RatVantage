# Code of Conduct

RatVantage uses a simple contributor standard:

- Be respectful and direct.
- Keep technical criticism about the work, not the person.
- Do not harass, threaten, or attack contributors.
- Do not post private information, serial numbers, account details, or logs that identify someone without consent.
- Keep safety-sensitive hardware discussions factual and evidence-based.

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when behavior makes the project unsafe or unproductive.
